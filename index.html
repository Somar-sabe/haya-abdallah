Save Card on your Android Home Screen

Follow Guide

  1. Open chrome on your Android device and navigate to the Digital card you wish to add to your home screen.
  2. Tap on the three vertical dots (menu button) in the top right corner of screen.
  3. Select "Add to Home Screen" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Enter a name for the shortcut and tap Add
  5. The website will now be available as an icon on your home screen.
And thats it! Now you have a shotcut to your favorite website right on your home screen.

Save Card on your iPhone Home Screen

Follow Guide

  1. Open safari and navigate to the Digital card you wish to add to your home screen.
  2. Tap theShare at the bottom of the screen.
  3. In the share menu, scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Name the shortcut and tap Add in the upper right corner
And thats it! Now you have a shotcut to your favorite website right on your home screen.
